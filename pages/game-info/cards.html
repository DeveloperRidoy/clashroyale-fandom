Cards

Explore the vast collection of cards in Clash Royale, each with its own unique abilities and strategies. From powerful troops to devastating spells, these cards are essential for building your winning deck and dominating the battlefield.

Knight Troop 3 Common Medium The Knight is a versatile melee fighter with moderate hitpoints and damage, making him effective against a wide range of enemy troops.
