Arenas

“Arenas are the battlefields where players compete.”

source: Clash Royale Wiki

Arenas in Clash Royale are unique battlegrounds where players compete. As a player’s Trophy count increases, new Arenas unlock, each offering rewards like Chests. Higher Arena levels provide more Gold and Cards. Arenas also introduce specific features, such as TV Royale, separate Chest cycles, and the ability to buy Legendary Cards. Each Arena contributes to the game’s dynamic and strategic gameplay
