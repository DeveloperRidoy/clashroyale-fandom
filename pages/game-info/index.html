Champions

Champions are powerful and rare units that players can deploy on the battlefield to gain a significant advantage over their opponents. These champions are often legendary cards, and they possess unique abilities and attributes that make them stand out from regular troops.

Champions can turn the tide of battle with their special powers, whether it's dealing devastating damage, providing support to other troops, or controlling the flow of the game. They often have higher hitpoints, deal more damage, or have special abilities that make them highly sought after by players.

Deploying a champion at the right moment can swing the momentum of the game in your favor, allowing you to push for victory or defend against enemy attacks more effectively. However, champions usually come with a higher elixir cost, so players must manage their elixir resources wisely and choose the right moments to deploy these powerful units.
